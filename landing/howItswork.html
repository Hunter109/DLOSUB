    Frequently Asked Questions

    What cryptocurrencies can I use to purchase land?

    Ethereum, USDT, and other supported ERC-20 tokens can be used for property transactions.

    How is land ownership verified?

    Ownership is verified through smart contracts and digital tokens linked to verifiable property metadata stored on-chain.

    Can I co-own a land parcel with others?

    Yes, our tokenization model allows for fractional ownership, ideal for families or investment groups.

    Is it possible to transfer ownership across borders?

    Absolutely. As long as both parties are verified, land token ownership can be transferred globally without paperwork delays.

    What if a dispute arises over ownership?

    Blockchain records serve as legally verifiable proof. Disputes can be resolved through decentralized arbitration mechanisms.

    Can government authorities integrate with this system?

    Yes. DLOSUB offers APIs for municipal and national registries to synchronize and validate blockchain land records in real time.
